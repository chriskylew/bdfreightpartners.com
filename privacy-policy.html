Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

B&D Freight Partners respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through bdfreightpartners.com and related communication channels.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, location, application details, onboarding details, and communication preferences when you submit forms or communicate with us.

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, process applications, coordinate onboarding, send document reminders, provide scheduling updates, and communicate operational information.

SMS Communications

By providing your phone number through our application or onboarding forms, you consent to receive SMS messages from B&D Freight Partners related to application updates, onboarding, scheduling, document reminders, and operational coordination. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for support.

No Sale or Sharing of SMS Consent

We do not sell, rent, or share SMS opt-in data or SMS consent with third parties for marketing purposes. Information may be shared only with service providers as needed to operate our website, communications, onboarding workflows, or as required by law.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information submitted to us. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP or contact us at onboarding@bdfreightpartners.com.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact B&D Freight Partners at onboarding@bdfreightpartners.com.
