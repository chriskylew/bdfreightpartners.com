Privacy Policy

B & D Freight Partners respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide through this website. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide through our forms, including your name, phone number, email address, and other information related to prequalification or application submissions.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to review your submission, respond to your inquiry, communicate about potential opportunities, and send follow-up messages related to your application or request.

SMS Communications

If you provide your phone number, you may receive SMS messages related to your inquiry, prequalification, application, or customer support. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

Email Communications

If you provide your email address, we may use it to send follow-up communication related to your inquiry, application, or requested information.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may use service providers only as needed to operate our website, messaging, and communication systems.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Your Choices

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP. If you need assistance, you may reply HELP where supported or contact us through our website.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact B & D Freight Partners through the website contact or application process.
